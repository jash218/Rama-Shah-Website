Who is Rama Harshad Shah?

Smt. Rama Harshad Shah, a Teacher, a Business Woman, Philanthropist, Editor and writer has till today written and published 180 no. of books of Poetries, Short stories, Autobiography and Haikus. She lost both her parents at a tender age of 14 leaving behind 5 siblings to be taken care by her. Taking the misery in her stride, in a year’s time she took up a job of teacher in school and alongside she pursued her studies as an external student to be graduate & post graduate. She married Shri Harshad Shah who had just started his business of Scientific instruments and to expand the business she joined him. The venture proved successful, hence they thought of repaying to the community and started donations in various activities at Community Centre, Hospitals etc. Nearing the age of 60 on peak of their business they voluntarily decided to opt for retirement. Adding another feather to her cap, she decided to start writing Poems at the age of 65 in her mother tongue Gujarati. With the noble idea of sharing her feelings, thoughts and emotions with relatives, friends etc. she published her writings and circulated books free of cost. She believes that the ultimate goal of life is sadhana of “PARAM, the almighty God”. Hence title of her all books are starting with letter ‘P’ for Param such as Pratiksha, Prasadi, Parjanya etc., She has been awarded Certificate of Record for five times for the unique distinction of highest no. of books having title starting with same letter by Limca Book of Records for 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016 & 2017. In 2022 she was introduced to Haikus (the Japanese styled 3 liner poems) and she adapted it’s pattern. By 2024 she has written and published 25 books containing approx. 50,000 Haikus on various subjects such as Shri Ramayan, Shrimad Bhagwat, Krishna Charitra, Mahabharat, Shrimad Bhagwad Gita, Shiv Charitra, Mahavir Charitra, Shrimad Vallabhacharya Charitra, Buddha Charitra etc. This should be a World record of it’s kind as no one has so far written so many Haikus. She feels being blessed by Maa Saraswati and Maa Laxmi both. Her work is acknowledged by various leading Gujarati newspapers and magazines such as Janmabhoomi, Mumbai Samachar, Mid-Day, Chitralekha etc. Due to her philanthropic work she has been awarded Lad Ratna and many awards by her community centres. At the age of 92 she is actively working for betterment of society as President of Vishwa Lad Parishad, which provides Educational, Medical help to needy and runs Sanatorium facility at various locations. Her Achievements and story of life is an inspiration to others.

Her Journey

Rama's journey as a writer began in her early years when she discovered her passion for storytelling. Over the years, she has honed her craft and created works that resonate with readers across cultures. Her dedication to literature is evident in her ability to weave relatable characters and compelling narratives that leave a lasting impression.
